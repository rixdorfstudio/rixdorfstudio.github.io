About Sudoku — Classic Number Puzzle

Sudoku — Classic Number Puzzle is a beautiful, focused Sudoku app for iOS. It's free to download with an optional one-time Premium upgrade. No ads, no tracking, no subscriptions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock Hard & Expert?

Hard and Expert difficulty require a Premium upgrade (€4.99, one-time). Tap "Upgrade to Premium" on the home screen to unlock all features forever.

I lost my purchase — how do I restore it?

Go to the Upgrade screen and tap "Restore Purchase". Your previous purchase will be restored automatically via Apple.

What is Klara Mode?

Klara Mode is a special personalised mode. It can be activated with a secret PIN and unlocks all features for free.

How does the Daily Challenge work?

Every day at midnight, a new Medium puzzle is available. The same puzzle is generated for every device using the same daily seed — so everyone plays the same challenge each day.

My hearts are gone — can I get them back?

When you run out of hearts (3 mistakes), you can restore all 3 hearts for €0.49 — or start a fresh game. In Klara Mode, hearts can be restored for free.

Does the app collect any data?

No. Sudoku — Classic Number Puzzle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
