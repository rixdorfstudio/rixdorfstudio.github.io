What We Stand For

The world deserves
better mobile games.

The mobile market is dominated by manipulative mechanics, aggressive monetization, and disposable experiences. We are building the alternative — and these are the principles we don't compromise on.

01
No Dark Patterns
No fake urgency, no pay-to-win, no manipulative mechanics. One fair price, and you own the game. Always transparent about what you're getting.
02
Privacy First
No accounts, no ads, no personal data collected. Your game progress lives on your device — and stays there.
03
Accessible from Day One
Four languages, adaptive layouts, interactive tutorials, and thoughtful UX — so every player feels welcome.
04
Built to Last
Offline-first, no bloated SDKs, clean native SwiftUI architecture — maintained and improved long after launch.

“The best mobile games don't feel like products.
They feel like time well spent.”

The Studio

Small studio. Big ambition.

Rixdorf Studios is an independent game studio based in Berlin, founded in 2026 by Bruno Herzog. We believe excellent games and honest business can coexist.

The studio takes its name from Rixdorf — the historic village at the heart of Berlin-Neukölln. Like its namesake, the studio values craft over noise: every game is designed, built, and polished in-house, on a shared native foundation that lets a small team ship at remarkable speed without ever cutting corners.
