Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Rixdorf Studios builds apps that respect your privacy. Our apps do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

Our apps do not collect any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking.

All game data (settings, progress, statistics) is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to those transactions. We do not receive any personal data from these transactions beyond what Apple provides to developers by default.

Children

Our apps are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children or any users.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.
